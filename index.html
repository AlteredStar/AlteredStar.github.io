Welcome to Character Creation!

There are 9 attributes that you may increase through your decisions.


Health represents your physical body's condition. A steadfast nature that actively seeks adventure increases your health.
Weapons attuned to heatlh are gauntlets and quarter staffs.


Mana represents your capacity to handle magic. An inquisitive nature that pursues discovery of the unknown increases your mana.
Weapons attuned to mana are orbs and talismans.


Strength represents your physical prowess. A bold nature that challenges problems with force increases your strength.
Weapons attuned to strength are greatswords, axes, and hammers.


Vitality represents your ability to absorb hits. A determined nature that remains unwavering regardless of the situation increase your vitality.
Weapons attuned to vitality are shields and maces.


Dexterity represents your ability to control your body and handle tools. A energetic nature that strives for growth.
Weapons attuned to dexterity are daggers, shortswords, bows, and pole-arms.


Intelligence represents your potential to learn. A witty nature that picks up knowledge and skills quickly increases your intelligence
Weapons attuned to intelligence are scepters and wands.


Wisdom represents your judgement and experience. A mature nature that grasps that complexities of life increases your wisdom.
Weapons attuned to wisdom are staves and tomes.


Spirit represents your resistance to mental and status effects. A virtuous nature that upholds strong morals increases your spirit.
Weapons attuned to spirit are crosses and blades.


Charisma represents your charm as perceived by other entities. An outgoing nature that emanates kindness inreases your charisma.
Weapons attuned to charisma are longswords, flags, and horns.


Question 1

You are walking along a path in order to find essentials for the upcoming harsh winter season and as the sun begins, you come across a fork in the road. The left path leads deeper into the forest where the existence of wolves is certain, but you will reach a wealthy city within 3.5 hours. The right path leads to a clearing where a dim mansion can be seen in the distance, you estimate the distance to be about 2.5 hour away. Behind you is a straight path leading back home, and you may rest at a town 1.5 hours away along the path. Next to the crossroad is a small clearing fit for setting up camp. Which path do you take?











Question 2

During a trip to the market, you find a pouch on the ground containing a handful of gold coins. You may give the pouch to the nearby guards who have a reputation for being fair to the citizens of your town. You may leave the pouch where it lies. You may take the gold for yourself. You may give the pouch to the local orphanage where you have aquainted yourself with its director.











Question 3

You have been transported into a fantasy world. You open your eyes in the King's audience chamber. The Kingdom desperately pleas for your help to defeat the Demon Lord. You may wholeheartedly agree to help the poor Kingdom, and your journey to defeat the Evil Demon Lord will begin. You may agree to their request and decide your true alligance later, and regardless of whether or not you fight for yourself, the Kingdom, or against the Kingdom, your journey to defeat the Demon Lord will begin. You may decieve the Kingdom and runaway during the night after gathering information in the Palace, but you will become a wanted criminal in the Kingdom. You may attempt to cajole the Princess in hopes of strengthening your actual influence in the Kingdom; however, success is not guaranteed, and you must stay allied with the Kingdom at least until your efforts have yielded a positive gain.
